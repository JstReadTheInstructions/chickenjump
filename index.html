Chicken Jump

How to Play:

  • Press W to jump.
  • Press A or D to dash when the dash bar appears.
  • Avoid obstacles and keep running as far as you can!
  • Game ends if you hit an obstacle.
  • Press R to restart after a game over.

Watch out for faster speeds as you progress!
